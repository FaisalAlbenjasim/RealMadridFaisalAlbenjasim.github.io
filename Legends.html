Legends of Real Madrid CF

Real Madrid is very famous for the legends that played for team's logo, they are now considered as icons that Madridistas (Real madrid fans) are so greatful for them to win them trophies and tournaments. Below is a list of the top 10 legends to ever play for the Whites.

Legends Description Image
Alfredo Di Stefano Argentine-national player that is considered as one of the best attackers in football history.
Zinedine Zidane French nationality player that is considered as one of the greastest players of all time.
Raul Gonzalez Spanish nationality attacking player that has be named as one of the top scorers of the club.
Iker Casillas Spanish nationality who is considered to be one of the greatest goalkeeper in football.
Sergio Ramos Spanish nationality who is considerd among the best defenders in football.
Cristiano Ronaldo Portuguese nationality attacker who is considered to be the greatest football player of all time and the top goal scorer of the club.
Ferenc Puskas Hungarian nationality attacker who is regarded as one of the ggreatest player of all time.
Roberto Carlos Brazilian nationality full back and was one of the best set-piece takers in football.
Luka Modric Croatian nationality playmaker who is considered as one of the greatest players to play in midfiled.
Karim Benzema French nationality who has been considered as one of the best attackers in the club's history.

These legends of this gigantic club all contributed in making the club one of the best in world attracting many fans from all around the world.
