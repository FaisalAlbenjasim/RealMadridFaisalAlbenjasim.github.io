The Santiago Bernabeu

Real Madrid has a home stadium called "The Santiago Bernabeu" located at the spanish capital Madrid. The Santiago bernabeu is regarded as the second largest stadium in spain with a seating capacity of more than 80,000 attenders.

This stadium is really famous for its advance technology usage as this stadium can host different sports ocassions not only football.

It is vital that all Real Madrid fans need to know that they have the chance to access the world's most amazing sport venues and the musuem. This is where the fans can learn more about the history of Real Madrid and experince the transformation of the renovated Santiago Bernabeu.
